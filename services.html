PARTNERSHIP

We believe in working together different stakeholders especially the grass root communities to share knowledge, perspectives and ideas into environmental conservation efforts. This is intended to build mutual beneficial relationships among sectors from communities, government, NGOs to leverage influence and expertise.

INNOVATION

The current climate crisis is not just a problem to be solved, but also an opportunity to build a new way of being in this world. We encourage creativity by supporting sustainable practises and supporting research as a solution to our contemporary issues.

ACCOUNTABILITY

We are open to scrutiny and strongly believe that we can do better by being responsible and accountable to ourselves and the community we serve.

PARTICIPATION

Inclusive and meaningful participation is at the centre of our planning and activities. We encourage and promote meaningful and inclusive participation of diverse stakeholders in the execution of our work.
