ABOUT NATA

NATA is a non-profit organization located in Hoima district, Mid-western region Uganda. The organization started in 2022 to contribute to efforts of promoting climate justice, nature conservation, protection and preservation. We work through community convening, advocacy, key stakeholders engagement, partnership and collaboration with other likeminded organizations.
